🕹️ Tag – Play Local Tag Game with Friends

📜 Game Rules:

  • One player starts as “It” (the tagger).
  • Other players must avoid being touched.
  • If “It” touches another player, the tag is passed to them.
  • The winner is the player who spent the least time being "It".

⚙️ Game Settings (before playing):

    ⏱ Round Time – set how long the match lasts (e.g., 60, 120, or 180 seconds). 💨 Buffs – enable or disable power-ups like speed boost, jump pads, teleporters, and bumpers. 🌍 Map – choose from different maps, each with unique traps and layouts.

🎮 Features:

    Play on the same device (split keyboard). Great for friends or family. Simple controls: move with WASD / arrow keys, jump with spacebar.
